📝 What We Need

Simple Registration

We just need a few basic details about you and your YouTube channel. That's it — no technical setup required on your end.

1

Your Basic Info

Your name, email address and a short bio about yourself and your channel. This is what viewers will see on your creator profile.

2

Your YouTube Channel

Your YouTube channel URL, channel name and Channel ID. We use this to link viewers directly to your channel.

3

Your YouTube API Key

A YouTube Data API v3 key so we can display your latest uploads and video thumbnails in real time on your creator profile. Your key is stored securely and only used to fetch your public video data.

4

Your Content Focus

Tell us whether your channel focuses on Anime, Manga or Novels — and which genres or series you cover. This helps us match you with the right viewers.

5

Admin Approval

Our admin team reviews your application within 2–3 business days. Once approved, your channel goes live on the platform.
